What is Small Wins Tracker?

Small Wins Tracker is a simple and motivational website designed to help users track their daily or weekly achievements — no matter how big or small. Whether you finish a workout, read a book, or just get out of bed early, every small win counts. This site encourages positive habits, builds consistency, and celebrates your progress.
